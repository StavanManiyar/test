Admissions

Join Our Community of Learners and Achievers

Thank you for considering Kapadia High School for your child's education. We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can develop academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our admissions process is designed to ensure that we can meet the educational needs of each student and that our school is the right fit for your family. We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds who demonstrate a desire to learn and grow.

Admissions Process

A step-by-step guide to joining Kapadia High School

1

Inquiry and School Visit

Begin your journey by contacting our admissions office to schedule a campus tour. This visit will give you and your child an opportunity to explore our facilities, meet our staff, and get a feel for our school culture.

Available throughout the year
Duration: Approximately 1 hour
2

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with the required documents. The application form can be obtained from our admissions office or downloaded from our website.

Deadline: March 31st for the upcoming academic year
Application fee: ₹500
Download Application Form
3

Entrance Assessment

Students applying for grades 6-12 are required to take an entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness. The assessment covers English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge appropriate for the grade level.

Conducted in April
Duration: 2 hours
4

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and their parents will be invited for an interview with the school principal or admissions committee. This interaction helps us understand the student's interests, aspirations, and how we can support their educational journey.

Scheduled in April-May
Both parents and student should attend
5

Admission Decision

Admission decisions are communicated to parents via email and post. The decision is based on the student's academic record, entrance assessment results, interview, and availability of seats.

Announced by May 15th
6

Fee Payment and Enrollment

Upon acceptance, parents are required to pay the admission fee and first term tuition to secure their child's place. Once the payment is confirmed, the enrollment process is completed with the issuance of the school ID and other necessary materials.

Due within 15 days of acceptance
Multiple payment options available

Required Documents

Please ensure you have the following documents ready for submission

Birth Certificate

Original and photocopy of the student's birth certificate issued by the Municipal Corporation or equivalent authority.

Previous School Records

Report cards and transfer certificate from the previous school attended (if applicable).

Passport-sized Photographs

Four recent passport-sized color photographs of the student and two each of the parents/guardians.

Address Proof

Any government-issued document as proof of residence (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Utility Bill, etc.).

Medical Certificate

A medical certificate from a registered physician stating that the student is in good health and has received all necessary vaccinations.

Parent ID Proofs

Photocopies of ID proofs of both parents/guardians (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, etc.).

Scholarships

We believe that financial constraints should not hinder quality education

Merit Scholarship

Awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance in the entrance assessment or previous academic records.

  • Coverage: Up to 50% of tuition fees
  • Eligibility: Score above 90% in entrance assessment or previous academic year
  • Renewal: Maintain an average of 85% or above in all subjects

Need-based Financial Aid

Provided to students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds who demonstrate academic potential and determination.

  • Coverage: Up to 75% of tuition fees
  • Eligibility: Based on family income and financial need assessment
  • Renewal: Annual review of financial situation and academic performance

Sports Excellence Scholarship

Awarded to students who have demonstrated exceptional talent and achievement in sports at the district, state, or national level.

  • Coverage: Up to 100% of tuition fees
  • Eligibility: Represented at district/state/national level in recognized sports
  • Renewal: Continued participation and achievement in sports events

Arts and Cultural Scholarship

Offered to students who display exceptional talent in visual arts, performing arts, music, dance, or other cultural activities.

  • Coverage: Up to 50% of tuition fees
  • Eligibility: Demonstrated excellence in arts and cultural activities
  • Renewal: Active participation in school cultural events and continued excellence

To apply for a scholarship, please download and complete the Scholarship Application Form and submit it along with your admissions application.
